README- ECE 385 Final Project: Tetris with Power-Up Mechanics on the FPGA Board
By: Paige Nothelfer (paigetn2) and Upasana Halder (uhalder2)

How to Play:
1) Left Arrow: move the piece to the left
2) Right Arrow: move the piece to the right
3) Up Arrow: rotate piece
4) R Key: restart the game at any time

Scoring:
1 point for every row cleared

Pieces:
7 Standard TETRIS Pieces
1 special power-up block (white square): clears all the surrounding blocks in a 4x4 area around it
when it lands on a particular block

Plan ahead! The next piece is dislayed in a preview box to the right of the board
Game ends when the stack reaches the top of the board

Good luck, have fun!
